About 100 Yard Dash

100 Yard Dash is a random visualizer intended to help decide the draft order for your fantasy football league. There are two modes of Dashes to run (Random Dash and Code Dash), plus several customization options.

Random Dash

Each runner will rush between a minimum and maximum number of yards each rush, and each rush will last between a minimum and maximum number of seconds, and will then rush again at the end of the rush until they reach the endzone. For example, a runner could rush for 10 yards in 1 second giving them a big lead, or 1 yard in 8 seconds which will set them back.

Code Dash

Each runner will select a code, word, or phrase made up of a maximum of 20 letters, numbers, or characters. This code will then be converted into a series of rushes that each runner will rush. Now the randomness is in your hands, so choose your codes wisely.

Custom Images

Adding custom images will replace the rusher sprite with your linked image. Images must be a direct link to your image (links should end in .jpg, .png, or .gif). Shorter URLs are recommended to ensure a replay URL is created. Squared 1:1 ratio images work best.

Speed

There are four speed options to choose from.

Normal - Yards and seconds per rush are standard.

Fast - Yards per rush are doubled, seconds per rush remain standard.

Faster - Yards and per rush are doubled, seconds per rush are cut in half.

Fastest - Yards per rush are tripled, seconds per rush are cut into a third.

Luck

The optional Luck modifier gives a 5% chance per Luck point of doubling the runner's randomly rolled rush yards each turn. This can be used to give the previous season last place manager a better chance of winning, or to give everyone a bit of Luck to mix-up the Dash!

Settings and Replay URLs

A quick settings URL will be presented before the Dash that can be used to save custom images, manager names, and Dash settings. A replay URL will be presented after the Dash to watch the Dash again or share with your league. URLs are capped at 8192 characters. If your league has 20 managers with custom images, codes, and luck activated, the URL might be too long to watch the replay.
